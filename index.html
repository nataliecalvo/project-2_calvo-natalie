What is That App?

Have you ever wondered what you're looking at during a vacation? You know it's got to have a great story, but without a tour guide, you have no way of knowing what it is.

What is That? The Picture App can help. Just take a photo of what you're looking at, upload it and we will provide you with YouTube videos, articles and information about what you're looking at!

At 99 cents a month, it's a heck of a lot cheaper than a tour guide!

Here's How It Works

  1. Upload a photo to the app.
  2. Find out what you're looking at within seconds!
  3. Choose to watch a video, listen to a guide or read more about what you've discovered!
  4. Save the information for later so that you can reminice about your awesome vacation.
  5. Repeat!

About Us

When you submit an image to our app, we do a reverse Google Image search to gure out what you're most likely looking at. We then compile a library of resources we think will help you get the most out of your experience, like audio guides, YouTube videos and articles - all within 15 seconds! We're building a repository of places based on where are users most frequently visit so we can be even faster in the future!

Top Features

Our top app features are image recognition, location information and photo/content storage!

Location Information

We provide you with articles and videos to take the place of tour guides! Imagine having an expert at your finger tips to explain every step of your vacation. We use reviews from online and other users to determine what content you're most likely to enjoy for where you're at. Once you're done watching a video or reading an article, rate the quality of the content to help us figure out if it was helpful.

Image Recognition

Our first feature is image recognition. It's the basis of our app! You could Google information about what you're looking at yourself or save your photos on a social networking site, but you can't come up with a search query or social media caption without the name of what you're looking at!

Our app compares your photo to others on the web and uploads from other users to gure out where you're most likely to be. Information about where you're traveling helps us too!

Then we can give you the name of what you're looking at with 95 percent accuracy! Let us know when you think we're wrong, it will help us get better in the future.

A Scrapbook for Later

We'll store all of the cool things you looked at throughout your vacation with the information we gave you to read about it. That way if you want to refresh your memory or give a great explanation to friends and family, you can!

If all that sounds interesting, give our application a try by downloading it from the Apple App Store or Google Play!
